India's Exotic Safari and Royal Grandeur

Embark on a 17-day odyssey that blends the thrill of India's untamed jungles in Kanha and Kaziranga with the opulent charm of Kolkata, Jaipur, and Delhi. This journey is your ticket to an exotic rendezvous with India's wildlife and regal splendor.

Tour Details

Upon your arrival at either Mumbai or Delhi International Airport you will be met and cheauffered to your hotel. International flight tickets and visa arrangements should be coordinated directly at your end.

After a relaxing transit stay, this morning or afternoon, you'll be transferred to the airport for your flight to Jabalpur. Upon landing at Jabalpur Airport, a scenic 4.5-hour drive will take you to the enchanting Kanha National Park. Check in at your hotel and relish the serene surroundings. While there won't be a safari today, your stay includes delicious full board meals.

ABOUT KANHA NATIONAL PARK: It sprawls across 940 square kilometers in central region of India in the state of Madhya Pradesh. It's home to varied wildlife residents, including Royal Bengal tigers, sloth bears, Indian leopards, Barasingha, and Indian wild dogs. The area was historically governed by the Gond dynasty, and the forest was relatively unknown due to traditional slash-and-burn cultivation by Baiga and Gond tribes. The park being featured in Rudyard Kipling's "The Jungle Book". Forest type is moist Deciduous & Dry Deciduous Forest with river Banjar River, Hills, Valleys, Plateaus.

Embark on an adventurous day with both morning and afternoon Shared Jeep Safaris. A maximum of 6 guests per Jeep. Exclusive Jeeps are available at an additional cost, subject to availability and the discretion of the Forest Management.

In the morning or afternoon, embark on a picturesque journey to Raipur Airport, a drive of approximately 5 hours. From there, catch your flight to the vibrant city of Kolkata. Upon arrival, you will be warmly greeted and transferred to your hotel.

Discover the rich cultural tapestry of Kolkata, a city known for its grand colonial architecture, art galleries, and vibrant festivals. Explore iconic landmarks such as St. Andrew’s Church, the General Post Office, and the majestic Victoria Memorial. Your night will be spent in a centrally located hotel, allowing you to soak in the cultural vibrancy of this city.

After a delicious breakfast, you will be transferred to the airport for your flight to Jorhat. Upon arrival, embark on a scenic 3-hour drive to reach the captivating Kaziranga National Park. Check in at your hotel and enjoy a full board stay.

Experience the incredible wildlife of Kaziranga with morning and afternoon Shared Jeep safaris. Each Jeep accommodates a maximum of 6 guests, and exclusive Jeeps are available upon request, subject to availability and Forest Management discretion.

Embark on a guided tour of Hyderabad's attractions, including Golkonda Fort, Charminar, Mecca Masjid, and the vibrant Laad Bazaar. Don't miss a visit to the Salar Jung Museum and the Paigah Tombs for a dose of art and history.

ABOUT KAZIRANGA NATIONAL PARK : Nestled in Assam, a northeastern state of India, spans across 430 sq km. It's bounded by the mighty Brahmaputra River in the North and the Karbi Anglong hills in the South. As you drive through this region, you might even encounter rhinos and wild elephants wandering near the highway. This natural wonder is a UNESCO World Heritage site renowned for its population of the Great Indian one-horned rhinoceros. The landscape of Kaziranga boasts dense forests, towering elephant grass, rugged reeds, marshes, and shallow pools. It's a thriving habitat for a diverse range of wildlife, including tigers, elephants, panthers, bears, and countless bird species. What sets this park apart is the opportunity to explore it on elephant-back rides or take a tranquil boat cruise along the Brahmaputra River that borders the park.

Delve deeper into the natural wonders of Kaziranga’s wilderness.

This morning, embark on a scenic drive of approximately 4.5 to 5 hours to reach Guwahati Airport, where you will catch your flight to the magnificent city of Jaipur. Upon arrival, you will be warmly greeted and transferred to your hotel.

Discover the vibrant essence of Jaipur, often referred to as the "Pink City." Explore its wide avenues, lush gardens, and opulent palaces. Immerse yourself in the city's rich history and culture as you visit magnificent forts and bustling bazaars renowned for Rajasthani jewelry, textiles, and footwear.

After a delightful breakfast, embark on a 3.5-hour drive to reach Ranthambore National Park. Check in at your hotel and enjoy a full board stay. In the afternoon, set out on a thrilling Shared Jeep Safari. Each Jeep can accommodate a maximum of 6 guests, and exclusive Jeeps are available upon request, subject to availability and Forest Management discretion.

ABOUT RANTHAMBORE NATIONAL PARK: Once a favoured hunting ground of Jaipur's Maharajas, has now become an adored destination for wildlife enthusiasts worldwide. The park offers breath-taking panoramic views, especially from its historic fort, which stands as a testament to remarkable architecture, formidable fortifications, and captivating history. Visiting this fort is an absolute must when in Ranthambore. Nestled in the embrace of the majestic Aravali and Vindhya mountain ranges, Ranthambore National Park covers a sprawling 392 sq km, with 282 sq km dedicated to the core area and the remainder as the buffer zone. As you explore, keep your eyes peeled for the park's most celebrated inhabitants, including tigers, leopards, striped hyenas, caracals, sloth bears, and elegant blackbucks, to name just a few.

Experience the enchanting wildlife of Ranthambore.

After a leisurely breakfast or lunch, embark on a scenic drive to reach Delhi Airport, approximately 5 hours away. From there, you can catch your onward international flight connection back home.

Prepare for your journey back home from either Delhi or Mumbai, taking with you cherished memories of your incredible Indian wildlife adventure.

Please note that while we strive to provide you with the best wildlife experiences, tiger sightings remain unpredictable. Embrace the wilderness with an open heart. Keep in mind that all parks are closed for safaris every Wednesday.
