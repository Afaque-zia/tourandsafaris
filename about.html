Story

With a Post Graduate degree in Management and Marketing, my career has spanned diverse roles that have enriched my understanding and commitment to curating travel experiences that meet the diverse requirements and tastes.

I began my journey as a trainee at TRADE WINGS INDIA LTD, contributing to the organization of major international cricket tournaments. This spirited start laid the foundation for a career devoted to understanding and meeting travelers' specific needs and desires. My role as a company representative at ABERCROMBIE & KENT India office provided invaluable insights into guest requirements and the intricacies of facilitation.

Transitioning to KUONI DESTINATION MANAGEMENT, I further refined my skills and streamlined operations, ensuring elevated product quality and guest satisfaction. This journey led to my tenure at INDIA SAFARIS & TOURS (& BEYOND), where I drew on my accumulated wisdom to ensure guests experienced the essence of India, Sri Lanka, Bhutan, Nepal, and the Maldives.

Throughout these experiences, I took pride in contributing effectively in increasingly senior management positions, solidifying my expertise in the travel industry.

I invite you to join me on this extraordinary adventure, where your aspirations take center stage. Welcome to "Tour & Safaris," where your journey becomes a tapestry of unforgettable stories, crafted with precision and heartfelt dedication.
