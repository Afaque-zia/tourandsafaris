Taste of Rajasthan

Where Royal Splendor Meets Rustic Charm

Embark on a mesmerizing journey through India's cultural and historical gems. It all begins in Delhi, where you'll delve into the city's rich history and modern vibrancy.

Witness the breathtaking Taj Mahal in Agra and continue to Jaipur, the Pink City, to explore the majestic Amber Fort and the opulent City Palace, topped off with a sumptuous dining experience at Suvarna Mahal. Dive into the Blue City of Jodhpur,

immersing yourself in local traditions and bustling markets. Discover the charms of Rohetgarh, including a village safari and Marwari horse riding. En route to Udaipur, marvel at the exquisite Ranakpur Temple complex. Conclude your adventure in Udaipur, the City of Lakes, exploring its regal beauty by the serene Lake Pichola. Tour Details

Your adventure commences as you touch down at Delhi airport. Warm smiles welcome you, and your chauffeur will drive you to your hotel.

Begin your day with a sumptuous breakfast. Venture into Old Delhi, where history breathes through ancient streets. Explore the colossal Jama Masjid, a testament to Mughal opulence. Enjoy a rickshaw ride or leisurely walk through Chandni Chowk, where history and modern life coalesce in a unique tapestry.

Visit Humayun's Tomb, a masterpiece in red sandstone and white marble, a precursor to the iconic Taj Mahal. Experience devotion at Gurudwara Bangla Sahab, a Sikh place of worship with a community kitchen serving all. Drive through colonial New Delhi, passing the Rashtrapati Bhawan (Presidential Residence) and India Gate. Pay tribute to Mahatma Gandhi at serene Gandhi Smriti.

Embark on a picturesque drive to Agra, home to the world's most extravagant symbol of love, the Taj Mahal. Explore this enchanting monument, and your night's accommodation promises a comfortable retreat.

Before the sun graces the world, behold the breathtaking beauty of the Taj Mahal. After breakfast, immerse yourself in the majesty of Agra Fortâ€”a testament to Mughal grandeur, vividly bringing the bygone era to life.

Board on a picturesque journey to Jaipur, the Pink City, where vibrant colors, bustling bazaars, and the majestic Amber Fort eagerly await your arrival.

Post-breakfast, visit the Amber Fort, a breathtaking blend of Hindu and Mughal architecture. Enjoy a photo stop at Hawa Mahal, the Palace of Winds. The rest of the day is yours to explore vibrant markets or simply relax.

Explore the City Palace and astronomical observatory, and in the evening, indulge in a private dinner at Suvarna Mahal, Hotel Taj Rambagh Palace â€“ a culinary journey fit for royalty.

Continue to Rohetgarh, a land where horse riding is a way of life. The voyage offers glimpses of the region's deep culture and heritage. Rohetgarh welcomes you for three nights of regal living at a charming heritage hotel.

Dive into Jodhpur, the Blue City, with its majestic Mehrangarh Fort and bustling local markets. The heritage walk and vibrant local culture paint a vivid picture of Rajasthan.

Unveil the hidden gems of Rohetgarh with a village safari introducing you to the Bishnois, the true ecologists, and the local culture. Experience the unique cultural tradition of riding legendary Marwari horses.

Continue your journey to Udaipur, the romantic city. Along the way, make a stop at the Ranakpur Temple complex, a marvel of Jain architecture showcasing exquisite artwork beyond imagination. Delight in a scenic lunch at a heritage property nestled in the lap of nature.

Explore Udaipur with visits to the City Palace, the exquisite Jagdish Temple, and a serene boat ride on Lake Pichola, capturing the essence of the City of Lakes.

Enjoy a leisurely morning in Udaipur before taking a flight back to Delhi, where your Indian adventure began.
