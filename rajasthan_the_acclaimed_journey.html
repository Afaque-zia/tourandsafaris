Rajasthan the Acclaimed Journey

Where Royal Splendor Meets Rustic Charm

Discover exclusive Rajasthan with Taj on a 15-day adventure. Begin in Delhi, a blend of old and new.

Explore Udaipur's romantic charm, visit Kumbalgarh's massive fort, and marvel at Jain temples in Ranakpur. Explore Jodhpur's Mehrangarh Fort and experience Bishnoi village life.

Discover Jaisalmer's golden beauty, go on a desert safari in Manwar, and explore Pushkar's spirituality. In Jaipur, explore historic sites, ride elephants at Amber Fort, and head to Agra to witness the Taj Mahal at sunrise. This journey weaves culture, history, and natural beauty into an unforgettable experience.

Tour Details

Arrival at Delhi's International Airport and transfer to the hotel. Begin your journey in India's bustling capital city.

After breakfast and checking out from the hotel, explore the iconic Qutab Minar, a towering symbol of Delhi's history. Later, catch a flight to Udaipur, the City of Lakes, and settle into your hotel.

Start your day with a visit to the magnificent City Palace, showcasing the grandeur of Rajasthan's royalty. Wander through local markets, and in the afternoon, enjoy an exclusive boat ride on Lake Pichola, surrounded by the ancient city.

Embark on a picturesque drive to Kumbalgarh, home to a 15th-century fort known for its stunning architecture and massive walls.

En route to Luni, visit the mesmerizing marble Jain temple complex at Ranakpur, an architectural marvel with intricate carvings.

In Jodhpur, explore the formidable Mehrangarh Fort, stroll through the bustling Clock Tower area, and immerse yourself in the local markets. Experience the unique Bishnoi village safari, discovering their eco-friendly lifestyle.

Drive to the "Golden City" of Jaisalmer, known for its stunning fort and havelis.

Explore Jaisalmer's enchanting attractions, including the golden Jaisalmer Fort, ornate havelis like Nathmal Ji Ki Haveli, Salim Singh Ki Haveli, and Patwon Ji Ki Haveli. End the day with a mesmerizing sunset at the sand dunes.

Drive to Manwar and experience a thrilling Village Jeep Safari before settling in at the Manwar desert camp for an evening of cultural performances.

Drive through scenic landscapes to Pushkar, a holy town known for its spiritual significance and vibrant markets.

Explore the tranquil town of Pushkar before heading to Jaipur, the Pink City, where you'll discover the City Palace, Hawa Mahal, and the majestic Amber Fort. Enjoy an unforgettable Elephant Safari and dinner at Dera Amer.

Delve deeper into Jaipur's rich history with visits to City Palace and the intriguing Jantar Mantar observatory.

Drive to Agra with stops at Chand Baori, a stunning stepwell, and Fatehpur Sikri, an abandoned Mughal city of historical significance.

Marvel at the breathtaking Taj Mahal at sunrise, an enduring symbol of love. Explore Agra Fort, a red sandstone marvel.

Return to Delhi with your unforgettable journey through India's heritage and culture. Depart for your onward flight home.
