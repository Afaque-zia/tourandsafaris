Kerala's Wellness Retreat: Dutch History, Birding, Tea Estates, and Backwater Bliss

You will be warmly welcomed at Kochi Airport. Your adventure begins in Thattekad, where a secluded jungle experience along the serene Periyar River awaits. Customize your day with activities like birdwatching and kayaking before journeying to the misty heights of Munnar, famed for its tea plantations. Explore Munnar's tea estates, trekking routes, and the Tea Museum. Continue to Kumarakom for a houseboat cruise through Kerala's backwaters & romantic dinner setups. Then, unwind at Marari Beach, rejuvenating with Ayurveda spa experiences and vibrant village explorations. Lastly Return to Kochi, the Dutch Town for a cultural tour.

Tour Details

As you step off the plane and set foot in Chennai, you are not just entering a city; you are entering a living canvas, where history, culture, and spirituality converge in a breathtaking masterpiece. Your journey begins with a warm welcome at Chennai airport, followed by a transfer to your hotel. Chennai, often fondly called Madras, is not just a city; it's an artistic expression etched in time.

As you wake to the sounds of the forest, the day is yours to customize. Will it be birdwatching, archery, or perhaps a leisurely kayak down a serene river? The bird sanctuary, a haven for enthusiasts, beckons just a short distance away.

Embark on a mesmerizing four-hour drive to reach the misty heights of Munnar. This picturesque town, perched at an elevation of approximately 1,600 meters above sea level within the lush embrace of the Nilgiri Hills, is renowned for its rolling hills, all blanketed by the verdant carpet of tea plantations.

Munnar, a haven of exhilarating activities, invites exploration. Craft your day with guided plantation walks, tea tasting sessions, and local market adventures. Or, perhaps, choose the thrill of trekking routes and the historical insights offered by the Tea Museum.

Bid farewell to Munnar's cool embrace as you embark on a 4.5-hour drive to serene Kumarakom. Your chosen sanctuary, featuring traditional Malabar architecture, offers a gateway to the backwaters, traditional boats, and lush canals showcasing the life and people around it.

A houseboat adventure beckons, gliding through Kerala's enchanting backwaters on converted rice barges. Savor lunch on board. Indulge in rejuvenating Ayurveda massages, a window into India's ancient healing arts. As evening descends, how about an unforgettable romantic dinner with the scenic backwaters as your backdrop

Picture yourself basking in the sun by the tranquil ocean, where the rhythmic waves create a soothing symphony just for you. Alternatively, immerse yourself in the world of rejuvenation with a holistic Ayurveda spa experience. Let skilled hands work their magic, melting away the cares of the world and leaving you refreshed and renewed. it's a journey of self-discovery and revitalization.

You have the option to dive deep into the heart of this vibrant fisherman's village by hopping on a tuk-tuk. Feel the wind in your hair as you explore its winding streets, it's an adventure filled with colors, flavors, and the rich tapestry of local life.

Awaken to the soothing sounds of the ocean and a beachside breakfast. Your journey to Kochi unfolds amidst scenic landscapes. Choose your perfect eco-friendly haven for a luxurious night's rest. The evening offers an opportunity for a delightful cooking demonstration, an invitation to savor Kerala's renowned cuisine.

The vibrant city of Kochi beckons you to explore its rich tapestry of history and culture. Witness the iconic Chinese fishing nets as they cast their intricate silhouettes against the horizon. Step into the hallowed halls of St. Francis Church, a historical marvel that whispers tales of bygone eras. Venture to the Jewish Synagogue, a testament to the cultural diversity that has graced these shores. And let the Dutch Palace, adorned with mythological murals, transport you through time.
