Wild Encounters and Backwater Bliss

Embark on a fascinating journey through the natural wonders of southern India. It all begins with your arrival in Bangalore, the gateway to a world of pristine beauty. As you venture deeper into the heart of nature, you'll discover the untamed wilderness of Kabini and Wayanad, where India's magnificent flora and fauna exits in abundance. Amidst bamboo forests and coffee plantations, you'll embrace the spirit of Kerala. Your exploration continues in Kochi, where living heritage and modern vibrancy coexist seamlessly. Then, lose yourself in the paradise of Munnar's lush tea plantations before surrendering to the tranquil allure of Kumarakom backwaters.

Tour Details

Your extraordinary Indian journey begins with the grandeur of Bangalore Airport welcoming you as your international flight touches down. From the moment you step off the plane, the warm embrace of southern India envelops you. Your haven awaits â€“ a comfortable hotel where a cozy room, meticulously arranged, is prepared for your arrival, ready from noon. Your first night in India is a delightful stay amidst the modern charm of Bangalore.

Embrace the call of the wild as you bid farewell to the city, setting forth on a scenic drive to Kabini. This captivating journey carries you deep into the heart of nature's embrace. Your sanctuary for the next two nights is a Jungle Lodge, an oasis of comfort and authenticity. Here, your every need is anticipated, including full-board accommodations that immerse you in the untamed wilderness.

Today, prepare to embark on an exhilarating adventure into Nagarhole National Park, a veritable paradise for wildlife enthusiasts. While game drives are subject to nature's whims, the prospect of thrilling encounters with India's magnificent creatures is almost guaranteed. The sights and sounds of the jungle will leave an indelible mark on your soul.

After a hearty breakfast, your journey leads you to Wayanad, a region cherished by locals for its awe-inspiring blend of majestic mountains, emerald-green rice paddies, and lush plantations. For the next two nights, a Jungle Lodge in the heart of Wayanad's natural beauty awaits. Here, you are fully immersed in the vibrant tapestry of this captivating landscape.

Your exploration of Wayanad continues, promising encounters with magnificent wild elephants and a profound connection with the untamed side of Kerala. Amidst the serene bamboo forests and fragrant coffee plantations, you find solace and adventure intertwined.

Today's journey carries you to Kochi, a city steeped in history and culture, where the past and present coexist harmoniously. Kochi's captivating blend of ancient traditions and modern vitality will leave you spellbound. You'll indulge in a two-night stay in the idyllic Cherai Beach Area.

Delve into Kochi's rich living heritage as you visit the Dutch Palace, the Jewish Synagogue, and the storied St. Francis Church. As evening descends, embark on a delightful stroll to the iconic Chinese fishing nets, where you'll witness the timeless spectacle of sunset against the backdrop of these ancient marvels.

Embark on a scenic drive to Munnar, a mesmerizing hill station cocooned by lush tea plantations. Here, the landscape sprawls with emerald tea gardens, picturesque towns, winding roads, and breathtaking vistas. Your two-night stay in Munnar promises serenity amidst nature's finest.

Today's adventure whisks you away to Kolukkumalai, the world's highest organic tea estate, where time-honored tea-making traditions endure. Experience the allure of tea culture dating back to colonial times, savor different tea varieties, and witness the daily rhythms of plantation life.

After a leisurely breakfast, your journey unfolds to Kumarakom, a serene backwater haven. Here, the sheer beauty of swaying coconut palms, verdant paddy fields, and tranquil mangroves will captivate your senses. You'll enjoy two nights at a Backwater Resort, where tranquility reigns supreme.

Embrace the backwaters with a traditional houseboat cruise on Vembanad Lake. These iconic houseboats, known as "Kettuvallams," offer a unique and indulgent experience. Revel in picturesque views, tranquil waters, and the soothing embrace of nature.

As your unforgettable sojourn draws to a close, bid a fond farewell to the backwaters of Kumarakom. Your journey returns you to Kochi Airport for your onward flight. International flight arrangements should be independently organized, ensuring seamless alignment with your schedule.
